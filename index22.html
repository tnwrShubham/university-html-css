Courses We Offer

JC Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA, Faridabad offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes. YMCA Faridabad courses are offered in full-time and part-time modes. YMCA Faridabad courses are available in engineering, sciences, arts, humanities and social sciences, management and business administration and computer application and IT fields. YMCA Faridabad UG courses are B.Tech, B.Voc, B.Sc (Hons.), B.B.A, B.A, B.Sc and B.C.A. YMCA Faridabad PG courses include P.G.D, M.Tech, M.Sc, M.A, M.C.A, M.B.A and Executive M.B.A. The university offers YMCA Faridabad PhD courses in 17 specialisations. Students have to fulfill the eligibility criteria to apply for the YMCA Faridabad courses. YMCA Faridabad fee structure for B.Tech ranges from Rs. 3.35 Lakhs to Rs. 3.61 Lakhs.
